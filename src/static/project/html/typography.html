Annontated text

Text can be stressed for emphasis or marked as important. Text in documents may be marked as having been deleted or added or as being no longer relevant or accurate.

Text may also be marked due to its relevance in a particular context. It can be stylistically different or set off without conveying any special importance or relevance. Or it can be an unarticulated, though explicitly rendered, non-textual annotation.

Accordion element

Accordion (GUI)

The graphical control element accordion is a vertically stacked list of items, such as labels or thumbnails. Each item can be "expanded" or "stretched" to reveal the content associated with that item. There can be zero expanded items, exactly one, or more than one items expanded at a time, depending on the configuration.
